01 · Problem

Irrigation stress is found too late.

Raw satellite data exists. Daily irrigation decisions do not.

💧

Limited water

Central Asia's water budget is shrinking. Every cubic meter must be allocated to the field that needs it most — today, not next week.

🛰️

Late field detection

By the time NDVI drops in optical imagery, the crop is already stressed. We need leading indicators, not lagging ones.

📋

No priority ranking

Districts get blanket water schedules. There is no per-square-kilometer ranking to drive dispatch.

03 · How we get the data

Seven open Earth-observation streams, fused.

All public, all multi-decade — ingested through Google Earth Engine and harmonized to a 1 km grid.

Optical

Sentinel-2

10 m multispectral imagery for vegetation indices (NDVI, NDMI, NDWI).

Radar

Sentinel-1 SAR

Cloud-piercing backscatter to detect surface moisture and water bodies.

Rainfall

CHIRPS

30+ years of daily 5 km precipitation — our rainfall anomaly baseline.

Soil

SMAP

L-band passive radiometer soil moisture, gap-filled and downscaled.

Weather

ERA5

Hourly reanalysis: temperature, wind, humidity, radiation.

ET

WaPOR · MODIS

Actual evapotranspiration — the demand side of the water balance.

Hydrology

OSM + HydroRIVERS

Canal and river network used to compute distance-to-water features.
